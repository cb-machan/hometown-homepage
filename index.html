Top three activities to do at Wayanad

Visit Gurukula Botanical Sanctuary

Head off to Periya for a spectacular view of plants big and small endemic to the region.

See the Edakkal Caves

Step back in time and take a look into the era of the stone age.

Experience the Wildlife Sanctuary

Go for an elephant ride, a birding tour, a nature trail, or a jeep safari.
